Your Window to the World

Light bounces off everything you see and travels into your eye. The clear cornea and the lens bend that light and focus it onto the retina at the back.

Light → Cornea → Pupil → Lens → Retina → Brain

A tiny upside-down picture forms on the retina — and your brain flips it the right way up!
