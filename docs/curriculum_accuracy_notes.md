# Curriculum Accuracy Notes

This file records curriculum-specific accuracy checks for the Grade 9 Science learning app.

## Pressure Exerted by Solids

- Pressure formula: `P = F / A`
- Force unit: newton `N`
- Area unit: square metre `m²`
- Pressure unit: pascal `Pa` or `Nm⁻²`
- Increasing force increases pressure.
- Increasing contact area decreases pressure.

## Human Circulatory System

- Blood flows from the body to the right side of the heart, then to the lungs.
- Oxygenated blood returns from the lungs to the left side of the heart.
- The left ventricle pumps oxygenated blood to the body through the aorta.
- AB is the universal recipient.
- O is the universal donor.
- Rh⁻ recipients should receive Rh⁻ blood only.

Any scientific fact, formula, diagram label, blood-flow path, or compatibility rule must be checked against the official Grade 9 Science curriculum before release.
